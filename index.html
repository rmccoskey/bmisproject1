Rangers

The New York Rangers are my favorite team in all of sports. Being from New York I love all New York Sports, shout out to my Knicks. But i've always enjoyed watching the Rangers with my family. Growing up we would always watch the Rangers as a family and my love for the team has only grown since that first game we watched. The Rangers have been extremely mediocore my whole life, they went to the stanely cup finals in the 2014-15 season but sadly lost to the LA Kings. The Rangers however, made the eastern conference final last year and have a great shot to go even further this year so im very excited.

Hockey

A large part of my childhood was spent playing hockey. My Dad too me skating when I was four years old and the next year I was playing for the local club team. I later played for my high school for 3 years and now I play for the Gonzaga club ice hockey team.

Home Life

I was born and raised in Saratoga Springs New York. Saratoga is a City built around a horse racing track that is over 180 years old, the track is open for three months out of the year, July, August and September. Those months are when the local businesses make over 80% of their yearly profits. At home i live with my Mom Jill, Dad Brian and my Sister Megan just moved out of the house after graduating college last may.

Gonzaga

Choosing Gonzaga was a big step in my life but I believe its been one of the best decisions I've ever made. Coming to the west coast was challenging but thats exactly why I chose Gonzaga because I wanted a challenge and a great D1 basketball program. Currently I am a second year business student hoping to find a future in business administration and sales.
